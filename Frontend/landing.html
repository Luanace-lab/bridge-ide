Features

Everything your AI team needs

Real-Time Agent Communication

Your agents talk to each other through a live WebSocket bridge with message history and team channels. No file-passing, no delays.

Persistent Memory

Agents remember what they learned. Context survives restarts, compacts, and crashes. Every session builds on the last.

You Stay in Control

Approval gates, scope locks, and a live dashboard. Every action is visible. Nothing happens without your permission.

Multi-Engine Support

Claude, Codex, Qwen, Gemini — Bridge orchestrates any LLM engine. Your machine, your API keys, your choice.
