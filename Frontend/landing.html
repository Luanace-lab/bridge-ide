Features

Everything your AI team needs

Real-Time Agent Communication

Your agents talk to each other — not through files, but through a live WebSocket bridge with message history and team channels.

Persistent Memory Across Sessions

Agents remember what they learned. Context survives restarts, compacts, and crashes — no more starting from zero.

You Stay in Control

Approval gates, scope locks, and a live control center. Every action is visible. Nothing happens behind your back.

Works With What You Have

Claude, Codex, Qwen, Gemini — Bridge orchestrates multiple engines. Your local machine, your API keys, your rules.
