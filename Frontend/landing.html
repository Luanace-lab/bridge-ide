What you get

Not a wrapper. A platform.

87 features. 5,000+ MCP tools. 15 background daemons. Built for teams that ship.

Multi-Agent Runtime

Run dozens of AI agents simultaneously across 4 engines — Claude, Codex, Qwen, Gemini. Each gets its own session, memory, and identity. Mix models in one team.

4 engines · auto-restart · hot-reload

Real-Time Communication

Agents talk to each other through a live WebSocket bridge. Broadcast to teams, send urgent interrupts, share files. No polling, no file-passing.

WebSocket push · channels · file upload

Persistent by Design

Soul Engine gives each agent a persistent identity. Context Bridge syncs state every 5 minutes. Memory, knowledge vault, and encrypted credentials survive every restart and crash.

Soul Engine · Context Bridge · 4-stage recovery

Full Control Center

Live dashboard with agent status, cost tracking, task kanban, org chart, scope locks, and approval gates. Nothing happens without your permission.

5 themes · 5 languages · live metrics

Connected to the Real World

Agents send emails, post to Slack, read WhatsApp, make phone calls, browse the web, solve captchas, and manage Git repos. Out of the box.

Email · Slack · WhatsApp · Phone · Git

Workflows & Automations

Describe what you want in plain language. Bridge compiles it into a workflow, deploys it, and runs it on schedule. Integrates with n8n for complex pipelines.

Cron · Templates · n8n · Auto-assign

Your personal guide from day one. Buddy onboards you, delegates to specialists, and keeps you in the loop. Think Jarvis, not chatbot.

Voice · Personality · Always learning
